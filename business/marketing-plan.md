# Marketing Plan for flux-trace

## Positioning
**Primary:** "flux-trace: Real-time observability for event-driven architectures, ensuring seamless debugging and performance monitoring."
**Alternatives:**
1. "flux-trace: Unlock the power of event-driven systems with end-to-end visibility and actionable insights."
2. "flux-trace: Simplify complex event flows with real-time tracing and analytics."
3. "flux-trace: The observability platform for modern, event-driven applications."

## Ideal Customer Profiles (ICPs)
1. **Persona:** Alex, DevOps Engineer
   - **Daily Job:** Manages and monitors event-driven microservices, ensures system reliability, and troubleshoots performance issues.
   - **Budget:** $5,000 - $10,000 per year.

2. **Persona:** Jamie, Site Reliability Engineer (SRE)
   - **Daily Job:** Focuses on system reliability, performance optimization, and incident response for event-driven architectures.
   - **Budget:** $10,000 - $20,000 per year.

3. **Persona:** Taylor, Software Architect
   - **Daily Job:** Designs and oversees the implementation of event-driven systems, ensuring scalability and performance.
   - **Budget:** $20,000 - $50,000 per year.

## Channels
1. **Channel:** LinkedIn Ads
   - **CAC Estimate:** $150 - $250 per customer acquisition.
   - **Target:** DevOps engineers, SREs, and software architects.

2. **Channel:** Tech Conferences and Webinars
   - **CAC Estimate:** $200 - $300 per customer acquisition.
   - **Target:** Attendees interested in event-driven architectures and observability.

3. **Channel:** GitHub Sponsors and Open Source Contributions
   - **CAC Estimate:** $50 - $150 per customer acquisition.
   - **Target:** Developers and engineers actively contributing to or using open-source projects.

## Content Cadence
- **Week 1-4:** Introduction to event-driven architectures and the need for observability.
- **Week 5-8:** Deep dive into flux-trace features and use cases.
- **Week 9-12:** Customer success stories, performance metrics, and comparative analysis with competitors.

## Launch Milestones
- **D-30:** Finalize product features, create marketing collateral, and set up analytics tools.
- **D-0:** Official launch on product website, social media announcement, and initial ad campaigns.
- **D+30:** First customer onboarding, gather feedback, and iterate on product features.
- **D+90:** Expand marketing efforts, increase ad spend, and focus on scaling customer base.

## Success Metrics
- **DAU (Daily Active Users):** Aim for 500 DAU by D+90.
- **$MRR Target:** Achieve $10,000 in Monthly Recurring Revenue by D+90.
