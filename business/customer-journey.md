```markdown
# Customer Journey Map for flux-trace

## Aware
- **Trigger Event**: Developers or DevOps teams encounter issues with tracing and debugging distributed systems, leading to inefficiencies and downtime.
- **Friction Points**: Lack of awareness about advanced tracing tools, reliance on outdated or inefficient methods.
- **User Emotions**: Frustration, confusion, and a sense of urgency to find a better solution.
- **Opportunities to Delight**: Provide educational content (blogs, webinars) and case studies showcasing the benefits of advanced tracing tools.
- **Metric**: Number of unique visitors to educational content and case studies.

## Consider
- **Trigger Event**: Developers and DevOps teams start researching solutions to their tracing and debugging problems.
- **Friction Points**: Overwhelming number of options, difficulty in evaluating the effectiveness of different tools.
- **User Emotions**: Overwhelmed, cautious, and seeking reassurance.
- **Opportunities to Delight**: Offer free trials, demos, and detailed comparison guides to help users make informed decisions.
- **Metric**: Number of free trial sign-ups and demo requests.

## Try
- **Trigger Event**: Users sign up for a free trial or demo of flux-trace.
- **Friction Points**: Complex setup process, lack of clear documentation, and initial learning curve.
- **User Emotions**: Excited but cautious, eager to see results but concerned about potential difficulties.
- **Opportunities to Delight**: Provide a seamless onboarding experience with clear, step-by-step guides and responsive customer support.
- **Metric**: Time to first successful trace and user satisfaction scores during the trial period.

## Adopt
- **Trigger Event**: Users experience significant improvements in tracing and debugging efficiency and decide to adopt flux-trace.
- **Friction Points**: Integration with existing systems, cost concerns, and organizational approval processes.
- **User Emotions**: Relief, excitement, and a sense of accomplishment.
- **Opportunities to Delight**: Offer personalized onboarding sessions, integration support, and competitive pricing plans.
- **Metric**: Conversion rate from trial to paid subscription and customer acquisition cost (CAC).

## Expand
- **Trigger Event**: Users achieve success with flux-trace and look to expand its usage within their organization.
- **Friction Points**: Scaling the tool across teams, ensuring consistent usage, and managing costs.
- **User Emotions**: Confidence, enthusiasm, and a desire to maximize ROI.
- **Opportunities to Delight**: Provide enterprise-level support, custom training sessions, and volume discounts for larger deployments.
- **Metric**: Number of additional licenses purchased and expansion revenue.
```
